RubberDux is the moniker of English singer/songwriter Danny Goodwin. Now making music in San Diego, Danny began his music career in a small village in Hampshire, England, picking up the banjo at 7 years old and adding a variety of instruments since.

Inspired by greats like Paul Simon, David Bowie, and The Beatles, along with the chill vibes of Jack Johnson, Ray Lamontagne, and John Mayer, Danny crafts a blend of folk, acoustic, and soulful romantic melodies. He has a knack for taking familiar tunes, unraveling them, and weaving them back with his own unique touch.

In 2013, after winning a contest while travelling Cambodia, he was signed to a label and spent a few years played across Europe, Asia, and North America. Thanks to playing for audiences all over the world, what truly makes a RubberDux show special is the connection with the crowd. With a mix of cheeky tales, heartwarming stories, and playful tunes, Danny invites his audience into a shared experience of music and memories. In 2018 he released his debut album "Just Keep Walking" to rave reviews.

After taking a couple of years off performing during the pandemic, Danny moved to the USA in 2021 with a more intimate, focused sound and introspective lyrics that has captured audiences around the States.
